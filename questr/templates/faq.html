What is Questr?

Questr is a crowdsourced shipping platform. Essentially a courier company, but with a different mindset from that of traditional courier companies.

How is Questr different from other courier services?

Unlike traditional courier companies like Canada Post or UPS or FedEx, Questr's shippers get packages and deliver them to the destination directly without the hassle of going to local facilities, saving much time and labour, by breaking down the centralized logistics and letting the crowd deliver the packages.

Is Questr safe?

Safety is Questr's top priority. By utilizing a multitude of security measures, Questr is guaranteed to be not only just as safe, but also even more trackable and traceable.

How does Questr verify its shippers?

We have very strict policies when it comes to approving shippers, each one of them is interviewed and their documents are verified, including their driving records, criminal records, credit card, driver's license, etc.

How fast does Questr delivery its packages?

Once a shipper picks up a package, he or she does not need to go to a local sorting facilities but driving directly from the pickup locaiton to dropoff location. A package from Scarborough Town Centre to Square One Mall may only take fourty-five minutes if the traffic is reasonably good.

How much does a Questr shipment cost?

Questr offers three types of size options: Envelope, Box, and Grande, starting from $6 plus per-km fees.

Envelop, Box, Grande, what's the difference?

An Envelope package can be a pile of documents or a deck of posters.
A Box package can be anything that fits in a regular moving box.
Anything larger than that is a Grande.

Where is Questr currently available?

Starting from March 11th, 2015, Questr is available in the Greater Toronto Area.

How do I become a shipper?

Go to Contact Us and let us know you are interested. We will get back to you very shortly.

What kind of vehicle do I need in order to become a shipper?

A bike, a scooter, a car, anything that can get you around the city quickly.
